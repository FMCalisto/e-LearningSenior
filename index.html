Senior e-Learning Platform Project

This project aims to create an Online Learning for Elderly Platform under the User Centered Design class at Instituto Superior Técnico, Universidade de Lisboa - Portugal.

Lesson 1

Who Are the Users

When developing user centred products, one of the very first concerns is to identify and characterize all users and user groups that may have an interest, be involved in or afected by the products development and introduction into the market.

For this session students shall identify groups of stakeholders on the project outcome and justify their choice by the involvement and impact of the product on each of these groups.

Also, students shall identify the most relevant user groups and define and characterize their general user profiles.

This will be the starting point to define the set of users for a focus group, which will follow and take part in the project along the whole semester.
