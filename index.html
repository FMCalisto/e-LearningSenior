Senior e-Learning Platform Project

This project aims to create an Online Learning for Elderly Platform under the User Centered Design class at Instituto Superior Técnico, Universidade de Lisboa - Portugal.

Lesson 1

Who Are the Users?

For this session students shall identify groups of stakeholders on the project outcome and justify their choice by the involvement and impact of the product on each of these groups.

Project View details »

Lesson 2

What Users Want?

For this lesson students shall contact the different groups of users that were identified for the previous lesson and inquire them about the project.

Project View details »

Lesson 3

Building Cultural Probes

In this lesson students shall build a set of cultural probes and prepare to apply it to users from their focus group.
